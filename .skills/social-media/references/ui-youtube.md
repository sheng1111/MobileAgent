# YouTube Reference

Package: `com.google.android.youtube`

## App Traits

- Long-form and short-form video platform
- Shorts: TikTok-style vertical videos
- Subscriptions and notifications
- Comments and community posts
- Playlists and Watch Later
- Premium: background play, downloads, no ads

## Known Quirks

- **2025 landscape redesign**: Actions Pill contains Like, Dislike, Comments, Save
- **Shorts are full-screen**: Swipe up/down to navigate
- **Double-tap to skip**: 10 seconds forward/back
- **Mini-player**: Swipe down on video to minimize
- **Center (+) for upload**: Create video, Short, or Go live
- **Bell icon**: Notification preferences after subscribing
