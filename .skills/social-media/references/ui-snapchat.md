# Snapchat Reference

Package: `com.snapchat.android`

## App Traits

- Camera-first app (opens to camera by default)
- Disappearing photos/videos (Snaps)
- Stories: 24-hour photo/video collections
- AR lenses and filters
- Snap Map for location sharing
- Spotlight: TikTok-style vertical video feed

## Known Quirks

- **Camera is home**: App opens directly to camera
- **Swipe navigation**: Camera is center, swipe to other sections
- **Send To flow**: Create snap → tap Send To → select recipients/story
- **Hold to record**: Hold capture button for video
- **Double-tap to switch camera**: Front/back camera toggle
- **Snap streaks**: Consecutive days of snapping with friends
