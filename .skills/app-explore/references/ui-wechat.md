# WeChat Reference

Package: `com.tencent.mm`

## App Traits

- All-in-one messaging, social, and payment platform
- Moments (朋友圈): Social feed like Facebook
- Mini Programs: Apps within WeChat
- WeChat Pay for payments
- Official Accounts for content/business

## Known Quirks

- **Four tabs**: WeChat (chats), Contacts, Discover, Me
- **Discover menu**: Moments, Channels, Live, Scan, Mini Programs
- **Hold to record**: Voice messages
- **Top-right (+)**: Scan, Add contacts, new chat
- **Red packets**: Send money in chat via (+) menu
- **Recall messages**: Within 2 minutes of sending
- **Moments like**: Double-tap or tap heart in comment area
