# TikTok Reference

Package: `com.zhiliaoapp.musically` (International), `com.ss.android.ugc.aweme` (Douyin/China)

## App Traits

- Full-screen vertical video feed
- Swipe up/down to navigate videos
- Algorithm-driven For You Page (FYP)
- Short-form video creation with effects
- Duets and stitches for collaboration
- Live streaming

## Known Quirks

- **Double-tap to like**: Most common method (anywhere on video)
- **Right-side buttons**: All interactions in thumb-zone (avatar, heart, comment, bookmark, share)
- **Swipe left**: Opens creator profile
- **Pull down on feed**: Reveals search
- **Center (+) for create**: Opens camera/upload
- **Comments are bottom sheet**: Slides up over video
