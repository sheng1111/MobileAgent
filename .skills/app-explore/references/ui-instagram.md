# Instagram Reference

Package: `com.instagram.android`

## App Traits

- Photo/video sharing with filters and editing tools
- Stories: temporary posts (24h), shown as circles at top
- Reels: short-form vertical videos (TikTok competitor)
- DMs: direct messaging with disappearing messages option
- Feed: algorithmic timeline of followed accounts
- Explore: discovery page with trending content
- Bottom tab navigation + top action buttons

## Known Quirks

- **Create button location changed (2025)**: Now in top-left corner, no longer in bottom nav
- **Regional variations**: Some regions (India, Korea) may open directly to Reels tab
- **A/B testing**: UI layout may differ between accounts
- **Double-tap to like**: Works on posts and reels
- **Long-press for options**: On posts, comments, messages
- **Swipe navigation**: Left/right between tabs, up/down for reels
