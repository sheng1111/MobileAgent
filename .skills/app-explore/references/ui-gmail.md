# Gmail Reference

Package: `com.google.android.gm`

## App Traits

- Email client with threaded conversations
- Floating compose button (FAB)
- Hamburger menu for folders/labels
- Smart categories: Primary, Social, Promotions, Updates
- Swipe gestures for archive/delete
- Search with filters (from:, to:, subject:, has:attachment)

## Known Quirks

- **2025 update**: Docked reply bar at bottom of email view
- **Reply bar stays visible** while scrolling long emails
- **Swipe actions** configurable (archive, delete, snooze)
- **Undo send** available within time limit
- **Schedule send** option when composing
